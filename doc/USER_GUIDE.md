## Getting Started

### Creating Your Account

1. Visit **vendgros.com** or download the mobile app
2. Click **"Sign Up"**
3. Choose verification method:
   - **Email**: Enter your email address
   - **Phone**: Enter your Canadian phone number (+1XXXXXXXXXX)
4. Enter the 6-digit code sent to you
5. Complete your profile:
   - Account type (Individual or Business)
   - Business name (if applicable)
   - Notification preferences

### Account Types

**Individual Seller**
- Best for: Individuals selling surplus items
- Features: Standard listing creation, basic profile

**Business Seller**
- Best for: Restaurants, bakeries, farms, wholesalers
- Features: Business name displayed, higher trust indicator

## For Buyers

### Finding Items

#### 1. Search by Location

1. Go to **Browse** or **Search**
2. Enter your postal code (e.g., M5H 2N2)
3. Select search radius:
   - 5 km: Your immediate neighborhood
   - 10 km: Local area
   - 25 km: Nearby communities
   - 50 km: Extended region
   - 100 km: Maximum range

#### 2. Filter Results

- **Category**: Produce, Bakery, Dairy, etc.
- **Price Range**: Set min/max per piece
- **View Mode**: Grid or Map view

#### 3. Browse Listings

Each listing shows:
- **Photos**: Multiple images of the item
- **Price**: Per piece pricing
- **Quantity**: How many available
- **Distance**: How far from you
- **Seller Rating**: Star rating and review count

### Making a Reservation

#### 1. View Listing Details

1. Click on a listing
2. Review:
   - Full description
   - Price per piece
   - Available quantity
   - Pickup address
   - Pickup instructions
   - Seller information and ratings

#### 2. Reserve Items

1. Enter desired quantity (max per buyer limit applies)
2. Click **"Reserve"**
3. Review reservation summary:
   - Total price
   - 5% deposit amount
   - Balance due at pickup
   - Pickup deadline (48 hours)
4. Click **"Confirm Reservation"**

#### 3. Pay Deposit

1. Enter payment details (secure Stripe checkout)
2. Supported cards: Visa, Mastercard, Amex, Discover
3. Payment is processed immediately
4. You'll receive confirmation via email/SMS

### Pickup Process

#### 1. Before Pickup

- Check pickup address and instructions
- Note the 48-hour deadline
- Prepare exact change for balance payment
- Save your QR code or PIN

#### 2. At Pickup

1. Show the seller either:
   - **QR Code**: Display from your phone
   - **PIN**: 6-digit verification code
2. Seller scans/enters code to verify
3. Pay the remaining balance (cash or e-transfer)
4. Collect your items
5. Inspect items before leaving

#### 3. After Pickup

1. You'll receive a rating request within 24 hours
2. Rate your experience (1-5 stars)
3. Leave optional comment
4. Rating remains private until both parties rate

### Cancellations & Refunds

**Before Pickup:**
- Cancel anytime before pickup
- Full refund of 5% deposit
- Inventory returned to seller

**No-Show:**
- If you don't pickup within 48 hours
- Seller can report no-show
- Deposit is forfeited (no refund)

## For Sellers

### Creating a Listing

#### 1. Gather Information

Before creating a listing, prepare:
- Clear, well-lit photos (2-10 images)
- Accurate item description
- Exact quantity available
- Fair pricing (check similar listings)
- Pickup location and instructions

#### 2. Create Listing

1. Click **"Sell"** or **"Create Listing"**
2. Fill in details:

**Basic Information:**
- Title (3-200 characters)
- Description (10-2000 characters)
- Category selection

**Photos:**
- Upload 2-10 images
- First photo becomes cover image
- Drag to reorder
- Max 5MB per photo
- Formats: JPEG, PNG, WebP

**Pricing & Inventory:**
- Price per piece (CAD)
- Total quantity available
- Max per buyer (optional limit)

**Pickup Details:**
- Full street address
- Special instructions (buzzer, parking, etc.)
- Location is auto-geocoded

3. Click **"Submit for Review"**
4. Listing reviewed within 24 hours
5. You'll receive approval/rejection notification

### Managing Listings

#### View Your Listings

1. Go to **Profile** ‚Üí **My Listings**
2. See all listings by status:
   - **Draft**: Not yet submitted
   - **Pending Review**: Awaiting admin approval
   - **Published**: Live and searchable
   - **Completed**: Fully sold out
   - **Cancelled**: Rejected or removed

#### Edit Listings

- Click **Edit** on any listing
- Update title, description, price, quantity
- Changes to published listings may require re-review

#### Delete Listings

- Click **Delete** on draft/published listings
- Listings with active reservations cannot be deleted
- Deletion is permanent

### Handling Reservations

#### Notification

You'll receive notification when:
- Someone makes a reservation
- Deposit is paid (reservation confirmed)
- Pickup deadline approaching
- 48 hours elapsed (no-show reporting enabled)

#### Prepare for Pickup

1. Review pickup time preferences
2. Prepare items for collection
3. Have verification method ready:
   - Phone/tablet to scan QR code
   - Paper to write down PIN

#### Verify Pickup

1. Buyer arrives and shows QR code or PIN
2. In the app, go to **Reservations**
3. Select the reservation
4. Choose verification method:
   - **Scan QR**: Use camera to scan
   - **Enter PIN**: Type 6-digit code
5. System confirms match
6. Collect balance payment (cash/e-transfer)
7. Release items to buyer
8. Click **"Complete Pickup"**

#### Handle No-Shows

If buyer doesn't arrive within 48 hours:
1. Wait for deadline to pass
2. Click **"Report No-Show"**
3. Inventory automatically returned
4. Buyer's deposit is forfeited
5. You keep the 5% deposit

### Pricing Strategy

**Setting Prices:**
- Research similar items in your area
- Consider bulk pricing (lower per unit)
- Factor in time/effort to prepare
- Account for 5% deposit system
- Competitive pricing attracts more buyers

**Price Examples:**
- Fresh produce: $1-5 per piece
- Baked goods: $2-8 per item
- Dairy products: $3-10 per unit
- Bulk ingredients: $0.50-3 per kg

## Payments & Pricing

### How Pricing Works

**Per-Piece Pricing:**
- All items priced individually
- Total = Price √ó Quantity
- Clear, simple pricing

**5% Deposit System:**
- Buyers pay 5% upfront
- Remaining 95% paid at pickup
- Low barrier for buyers
- Commitment mechanism

**Example:**
```
Item: Organic Apples @ $2.00 each
Quantity Reserved: 20 pieces
Total Price: $40.00
Deposit (5%): $2.00
Balance at Pickup: $38.00
```

### Payment Methods

**Online (Deposit):**
- Credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex)
- Debit cards
- Secure Stripe processing
- Instant confirmation

**In-Person (Balance):**
- Cash (recommended)
- E-transfer
- As agreed with seller
- 95% of total price

### Refund Policy

**Eligible for Refund:**
- Buyer cancellation before pickup
- Seller cancellation
- Listing removed/rejected
- Item not as described

**Not Eligible:**
- Buyer no-show
- Buyer changes mind after pickup
- Buyer doesn't like taste/quality (inspection recommended)

**Refund Timeline:**
- Processed within 24 hours
- Funds returned to original payment method
- 5-10 business days to appear

## Ratings & Trust

### Rating System

**When to Rate:**
- After successful pickup
- Within 7 days
- Both parties can rate

**What to Rate:**
- 1-5 stars (whole numbers)
- Optional written comment
- Consider: quality, communication, accuracy

**Blind Ratings:**
- Your rating is hidden until both parties rate
- Prevents rating retaliation
- Encourages honest feedback
- Ratings become public after both submit

### Building Trust

**For Sellers:**
- Clear, accurate descriptions
- High-quality photos
- Responsive communication
- Timely pickups
- Consistent quality

**For Buyers:**
- Show up on time
- Communicate if delayed
- Bring exact change
- Respectful interactions
- Follow pickup instructions

### Trust Indicators

**Seller Profile Shows:**
- Average rating (out of 5 stars)
- Total number of reviews
- Account type (Individual/Business)
- Member since date
- Response time (future feature)

## FAQs

### General

**Q: What areas does Vendgros serve?**
A: Currently serving all of Canada, with focus on major urban areas.

**Q: Is Vendgros free to use?**
A: Yes! No fees for buyers or sellers. Stripe processes payment fees ($0.30 + 2.9%).

**Q: What can I sell on Vendgros?**
A: Food items, surplus produce, baked goods, and other bulk consumables. No prohibited items.

### For Buyers

**Q: How do I know the items are safe?**
A: Inspect items at pickup. All sellers are verified. Report any issues immediately.

**Q: Can I reserve items without paying?**
A: No, 5% deposit required to confirm reservation. Prevents no-shows.

**Q: What if I can't make the pickup time?**
A: Contact seller through the app to reschedule. Must pickup within 48 hours.

**Q: Can I get a refund if I don't like the items?**
A: Inspect before accepting. Refunds only for items not as described.

### For Sellers

**Q: How long does listing approval take?**
A: Usually within 24 hours. Check email/SMS for notification.

**Q: Can I sell prepared/cooked food?**
A: Yes, but ensure you comply with local food safety regulations.

**Q: What if buyer doesn't show up?**
A: Wait 48 hours, then report no-show. Inventory returned, you keep deposit.

**Q: Can I edit my listing after it's published?**
A: Yes, but significant changes may require re-approval.

### Payments

**Q: When do I receive payment as a seller?**
A: You receive the 95% balance directly from buyer at pickup. 5% deposit stays in escrow.

**Q: What payment methods are accepted?**
A: Online: Credit/debit cards. In-person: Cash, e-transfer (as agreed).

**Q: Are there any fees?**
A: No platform fees. Standard payment processing fees apply to deposit.

### Ratings

**Q: Can I see ratings before both parties rate?**
A: No, blind rating system prevents retaliation. Both must rate first.

**Q: What if I forget to rate?**
A: You have 7 days after pickup. After that, rating window closes.

**Q: Can I change my rating?**
A: No, ratings are final once submitted. Rate carefully.
